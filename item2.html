Predicting Home Values Using Three Types of Spatial Regression Models

In this study predicting median home values of census tracts in Allegheny County, PA, spatial lag, spatial error, and geographically weighted regression models were used to test if they can better account for spatial autocorrelation found in the OLS model residuals.
