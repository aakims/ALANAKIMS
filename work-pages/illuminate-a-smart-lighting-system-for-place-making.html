Illuminate: A Smart Lighting System for Place Making

Illuminate is a project completed for Sensing the City class at University of Pennsylvania. Envisioning a smart lighting system for better place-making, expanding the usefulness of public spaces around the city by activating them after sundown, we developed a arduino-run prototype using motion and light sensors.

Below spreads were featured in 2018 issue of PennDesign City Planning Journal PANORAMA
